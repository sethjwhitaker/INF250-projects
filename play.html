Play Now!

Here is a list of the top 4 sites where you can play chess. If you are brand new, don't worry. Most of these sites have lessons and practice sessions for beginners.

Rank Name Pros Cons
1

Chess.com
  • Lots of features
  • Excellent post-game analysis
  • Tactics and lessons for all skill levels
  • Free version has limited features
2

Lichess
  • Completely free and open source
  • Analyze games
  • Less features overall compared to other sites
3

Sparkchess
  • Analyze games
  • Learn and practice
  • Most features require payment
  • Site is not as polished as others
4

Chess 24
  • Hosted live events with grandmasters
  • Analyze games
  • Learn and practice
  • Most features require payment
  • More expensive than other sites
